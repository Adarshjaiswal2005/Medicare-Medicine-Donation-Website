About MediCare

Together, let's bridge the gap between surplus and need.

Our Mission

At MediCare, our mission is simple — to make life-saving medicines accessible to those who can't afford them. Every year, lakhs of unused medicines go to waste, while thousands of lives are lost due to the unavailability of those same drugs. We aim to collect, verify, and redistribute unused, unexpired medicines to the underprivileged across India.

We believe access to basic healthcare is a right, not a luxury. Through community participation and a secure digital platform, we're creating a network of care — one donation at a time.

How It Works

Step 1: Donate

Fill the donation form and submit your unused, valid medicines securely.

Step 2: Verification

Our team verifies expiry dates and packaging to ensure safety and compliance.

Step 3: Distribution

Medicines are delivered to verified NGOs, clinics, and individuals in need.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about medicine donation and how we help communities

What medicines can I donate?

Learn about acceptable medicine types and conditions

Accepted Medicines:
  • Unused, unexpired medicines in original sealed packaging
  • Prescription medicines with at least 6 months validity
  • Over-the-counter medicines in good condition
  • Medical supplies like bandages, syringes (unopened)
Not Accepted:
  • Expired medicines
  • Opened or partially used medicines
  • Controlled substances or narcotics
  • Medicines requiring refrigeration
How do you ensure medicine safety?

Our comprehensive safety verification process

1
Initial Inspection

Every donation is visually inspected for packaging integrity and expiration dates

2
Pharmacist Review

Licensed pharmacists verify authenticity and safety of all medicines

3
Quality Testing

Random samples are tested to ensure potency and effectiveness

4
Secure Storage

Approved medicines are stored in controlled environments until distribution

Can I request medicines for someone else?

Requesting medicines for family and friends

Yes, you can!

Request medicines on behalf of family members, friends, or anyone in need

Required Information:
  • Patient's name and contact details
  • Valid prescription or doctor's note
  • Proof of financial need (optional but recommended)
  • Your relationship to the patient
Simple Process:
  1. Fill out the request form with patient details
  2. Upload required documents
  3. Our team reviews within 24-48 hours
  4. You'll be notified of approval and pickup details
