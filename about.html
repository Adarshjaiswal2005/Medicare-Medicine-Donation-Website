About MediCare

Together, let's bridge the gap between surplus and need.

Our Mission

At MediCare, our mission is simple — to make life-saving medicines accessible to those who can't afford them. Every year, lakhs of unused medicines go to waste, while thousands of lives are lost due to the unavailability of those same drugs. We aim to collect, verify, and redistribute unused, unexpired medicines to the underprivileged across India.

We believe access to basic healthcare is a right, not a luxury. Through community participation and a secure digital platform, we're creating a network of care — one donation at a time.

How It Works

Step 1: Donate

Fill the donation form and submit your unused, valid medicines securely.

Step 2: Verification

Our team verifies expiry dates and packaging to ensure safety and compliance.

Step 3: Distribution

Medicines are delivered to verified NGOs, clinics, and individuals in need.
