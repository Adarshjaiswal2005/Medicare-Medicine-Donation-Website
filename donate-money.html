Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about donating to MediCare

How will my money donation be used?

Your donation is used to purchase medicines for emergency cases, cover transportation costs for medicine delivery, maintain our verification processes, and support awareness campaigns in rural areas.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes, we provide 80G tax exemption certificates for all donations. You can claim tax benefits under Section 80G of the Income Tax Act.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major payment methods including credit/debit cards, UPI payments (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), net banking, and digital wallets.

How do I know my donation is secure?

All payments are processed through secure payment gateways with SSL encryption. We never store your payment information and follow strict security protocols.

Can I set up a monthly recurring donation?

Yes, you can set up monthly recurring donations. Contact us through WhatsApp and we'll help you set up automatic monthly payments.

Will I receive updates on how my donation is used?

Yes, we provide monthly impact reports showing how your donation has been used, including the number of people helped and medicines distributed.

What is the minimum donation amount?

The minimum donation amount is â‚¹10. Every rupee counts and helps us provide medicines to those in need.

Can I donate in memory of someone?

Yes, you can make a donation in memory of a loved one. Mention this in the purpose field when making your donation.

How can I cancel or modify my recurring donation?

You can cancel or modify your recurring donation anytime by contacting us through WhatsApp or email. We'll process your request within 24 hours.

Do you provide donation receipts?

Yes, we provide official donation receipts for all contributions. These receipts can be used for tax purposes and are sent via email within 24 hours of your donation.
