Get in Touch With Us

At MediCare, we believe that every unused medicine has the potential to save a life. Our mission is to connect people who have surplus medicines with those who are in desperate need of them. We understand the importance of healthcare accessibility and the difference a single tablet can make in someone's life.

Our platform is designed with simplicity and impact in mind. Whether you're a concerned citizen looking to donate unused medicine or someone in need of assistance, our team is here to support you every step of the way. With our streamlined process, transparent system, and dedicated volunteers, MediCare is proud to be a bridge between generosity and need.

Donors can feel assured that their contributions are handled with utmost care. Each donated medicine is checked for expiry and safety, and then redistributed to NGOs, charitable hospitals, and individuals who have requested them through our platform. We adhere to strict guidelines and collaborate with licensed medical professionals to ensure everything we handle is both legal and safe.

For those seeking help, our website offers a simple form where you can request medicine according to your need. All requests are verified and handled discreetly. We maintain the privacy of every individual and strive to fulfill requests based on urgency and availability.

We have impacted thousands of lives across cities and rural areas. The kindness of our donors has enabled students, elderly citizens, mothers, and children to access critical medicines in times of need. What started as a simple initiative has now become a growing movement of compassion and shared responsibility.

In addition to our online services, we also conduct offline awareness drives, free check-up camps, and collaboration programs with health-focused NGOs. We believe that creating awareness about responsible medicine donation is equally important to expanding our reach.

Our address and contact details are always open for public access. You can visit us, call us, or simply send a message through our website or WhatsApp for any questions, suggestions, or collaborations. Every interaction counts, and we deeply value your feedback.

If you're a school, college, corporate, or NGO looking to collaborate, we'd love to hear from you. Let's create a future where no medicine is wasted and no life is lost for lack of access.

Thank you for believing in our mission. Let's make a change — one medicine at a time.
